About Us

'Resurrection's Kuweni' is an independent stage play that goes beyond the historical Vijaya Kuveni story and conducts an experimental study of the truth about Kuveni. It is staged for the first time in Sri Lanka according to contemporary performance methods through new theatrical methods, exploring innovative aspects of the art of theater. The special feature of this is the use of innovative acting and emotional dance, which are currently available in world theater, in theater, and the innovative use of music, singing and other ancillary arts.
